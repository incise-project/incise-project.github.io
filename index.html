THE INTERNATIONAL CIVIL SERVICE EFFECTIVENESS (InCiSE) PROJECT

The International Civil Service Effectiveness (InCiSE) project is a benchmarking and peer learning project based at the Blavatnik School of Government.

InCiSE in 2024 and beyond

Building on the foundations created by the 2017 and 2019 editions of the InCiSE Index, the School is re-establishing the project and aims to release a new edition of the Index in 2024. Alongside a new edition of the Index we will also be consulting stakeholders to review the conceptual framework behind the Index to ensure it is fit for the future. Read more.
